​Heart disease is the leading cause of mortality globally, taking approximately 17 million lives each year, also affection 1 in every 4 Americans.  There are numerious risk factors for heart disease: High blood pressure, unhealthy cholesterol levels, diabetes, and other clinical symptoms.



People with multiple risk factors could be benefited from early detection of the heart disease done by a machine learning model.  Machine learning algorithms, such as K-Nearest Neighbor (KNN) and Random Forest have been used to assists disease prediction and diagnosis.
